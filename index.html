06 · Questions

Asked often,
answered straight.

Will you write my assignment or dissertation for me?

No, and honestly, be careful with anyone who says yes. Every UK university treats ghostwritten work as misconduct, the checks for it get better every year, and in England selling it is actually a crime. What we do is everything short of that: planning, structure, detailed feedback on your drafts, proofreading, presentation polish. Students are usually surprised how far that takes a piece of work.

My deadline is this week. Can you still help?

Usually, yes. Feedback on a draft normally comes back inside 48 hours, and quicker turnarounds are often possible if you tell us the date in your first message. The honest caveat: the earlier you send something, the more rounds of improvement it can go through. A week gives us room to be genuinely useful; a night doesn't.

Which subjects do you cover?

Most of them. Business, law, computing, nursing, engineering, finance and the other usual suspects are listed above, but the real answer is: send the brief and we'll tell you straight whether we have the right person. If we don't, we say so rather than wing it.

How does the referral scheme work?

Simple: refer a friend and you earn 10% commission on their first piece of work — and they get 10% off it. They just enter your referral code when they message us (there's a box for it in the contact section), or mention your name in the chat. Want your own code to share? Ask us on WhatsApp and we'll set one up on the spot.

Is it confidential?

Completely. We don't publish client names, we don't use your work as marketing material, and we don't discuss who we work with. The only people who know you use MyWork are you and us.

How does applying to jobs on my behalf actually work?

You approve a shortlist of roles each week. For each one we write a CV and cover letter around that specific job description — a genuinely different CV per job, not one file sprayed everywhere — submit the application using your details, and file that exact CV version in a tracker you can open any time. Months later you can still pull up precisely what any employer saw; walking into an interview, you can re-read the exact CV that got you in the room. Interview invites come straight to you — from there, the interviews are all yours.

Do you understand visa sponsorship requirements?

Yes. If you're on a Student or Graduate visa we search against the licensed sponsor register, so you're not wasting applications on employers who can't hire you, and we plan the timing around your visa dates. We're not immigration advisers, so for the legal side we'll point you to someone regulated.

What does it cost?

It depends on the work, so we quote per brief rather than publishing a rate card. Whatever you send us, you get one fixed price back before anything starts, and that's the price. For software projects, payments are tied to milestones you can see running, and any change in scope is quoted before we build it, never billed after.

Where are you based, and can we meet in person?

We're based in London and work with clients across the UK. Most coaching and career work happens over video call; in-person sessions can be arranged in London.

How do I get started?

WhatsApp us — it's the one way to reach us, and the fastest. A couple of lines about what you need and your deadline is plenty to start with. We reply within one working day, usually sooner.
